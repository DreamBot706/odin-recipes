Chicken Burger

The chicken burger is one of the most famous fast food items in the world. Started originally probably in the United States and has gone on to become a world wide sensation and is my personal favorite fast food item

Ingredients :

  • Fried Chicken
  • Burger Bun
  • Mayonnise
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Steps to Cook :

  1. First fry the chicken after coating in corn flour
  2. Chop the vegetables as desired
  3. Arrange the various components in the order of bun, mayonnise, tomato, lettuce, cheese, fried chicken and the same way up
