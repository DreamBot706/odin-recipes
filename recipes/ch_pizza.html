Chicken Pizza

The chicken pizza is one of the most famous fast food items in the world. Started originally probably in the United States and has gone on to become a world wide sensation but unfortunately I prefer burgers over pizzas.

Ingredients :

  • Barbeque Chicken
  • Pizza dough
  • Cheese
  • Pickle
  • Corn
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps to Cook :

  1. First cut the pickles and the cooked chicken into smaller pieces
  2. Now Shape the dough into a circle using elite tossing skills
  3. Arrange the cheese, pickles, chicken and corn over the pizza dough and add some tomato sauce on top
  4. Put this into the oven and after 20 minutes, your pizza is ready!
